Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when using Spendly. This includes your name, email address, and financial transaction data such as expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect technical information like your IP address, browser type, and device information to ensure the service functions properly.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. We use your transaction data to generate spending summaries, category breakdowns, and financial insights. Your contact information is used for account management and to communicate with you about important service updates or security matters.

Data Storage

We store your data on secure servers with industry-standard security measures. Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service and as required by law. Financial transaction records may be retained for an extended period to maintain accurate personal finance history.

Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, and functionality like font loading (e.g., Google Fonts). These services only receive the data necessary to perform their functions. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy or data, please contact us at privacy@spendly.example. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data through this contact method.
